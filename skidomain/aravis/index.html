Aravis
 
The Aravis mountain range separates the Lake of Annecy and the high Alps of the Mont Blanc, with its summit, the Pointe Percée, at 2753 m. This magical mountain range is home to two marvellous ski resorts: La Clusaz and Le Grand-Bornand. Only 6 km apart, they have each developed ski areas of amazing diversity, offering more than 220 km of skiruns in total!
