Information[ Up ]

The Skicircus Saalbach - Hinterglemm - Leogang is one of the largest ski domains in Austria. It is situated in the eastern Alpes between Salzburg and Innsbruck, in the Salzburger region.

Skicircus Ski Domain Statistics :
· 200 km of ski slopes in total
· 71 ski slopes, between 836 up to 2096 meters above sea level
· 55 lifts
· 25 km of cross-country tracks
· 2 funparks with 3 half-pipes
· 1 lift-pass for the whole domain
· Snowmaking facilities on all main slopes (100 km)
